# Pricing meeting — notes

Date: 2026-03-04
Present: Mara Voss, Dee Okafor (growth), Sam Whitfield (eng)
Duration: 45 min

## Context

Follow-up to the H1 strategy memo (2026-02-17). Inputs on the table: the
Brightcart "too cheap to be serious" loss, the January loss to Reconlily on
credibility, and support cost concentration in sub-$1M accounts.

## Decision

- **Pro goes from $59 to $79/seat/month, effective 2026-04-01, for new
  customers.** Unanimous. Rationale: credibility with mid-market finance
  buyers matters more than conversion rate at the low end we don't want anyway.
- **Free trial shortens from 30 days to 14 days**, same effective date. Dee's
  data: 92% of trials that convert activate within the first 10 days; the long
  tail mostly generates support load.

## Grandfathering

- All existing customers keep $59/seat for **12 months** (through 2027-04-01),
  automatic, no action needed on their side. After that they move to the
  then-current price with 60 days' notice.
- Mara communicates this personally by email the week of 2026-03-09, framed as
  a thank-you to early customers, before anything changes on the website.

## Parked (explicitly not decided)

- **Annual-only tier**: interesting for cash flow, but too many moving parts
  while onboarding is being rebuilt. Parked until the Q3 2026 pricing review.
- **Usage-based component** (per-transaction pricing): Sam raised it, group
  agreed the metering work alone disqualifies it this year.
