# ICP

## Best fit

- E-commerce companies at **$5M–$50M GMV**, selling on 2+ channels — Shopify
  plus Amazon and/or wholesale (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Who actually buys (ICP)").
- Finance team of **2–8 people**; the buyer is the controller or first finance
  hire, not the founder (source: source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md,
  "Who actually buys (ICP)").
- Expansion signal: an account has a person whose job is month-end close.
  When the founder does the books, the account churns (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Who actually buys (ICP)").

## Wrong fit — confirmed

- Sub-$1M GMV solo operators: sign up via the app store, never finish setup,
  cancel inside two months. Stop optimizing listing copy for them (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Who actually buys (ICP)").
- The single Q4 2025 price-driven churn was a sub-$1M account — acceptable
  loss per the memo (source: source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md,
  "Churn — what Q4 taught us").

## Context

- Base as of 2026-02-17: 41 paying customers, $23.4k MRR, ~4 net new
  customers/month since November 2025; acquisition is the Shopify app store
  listing plus two podcast appearances, no paid channels (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Where we are").
