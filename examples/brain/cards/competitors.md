# Competitors

## Reconlily (incumbent, upmarket)

- Enterprise-grade, strong brand with VP-level finance buyers; onboarding
  reportedly 6–8 weeks; pricing starts around $1,500/month (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Competitive landscape").
- Ledgerline wins on time-to-value when it gets a demo (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Competitive landscape").
- Lost a ~$30M GMV deal to them in January 2026 on credibility, not product —
  a driver of the 2026-03-04 price raise (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Pricing signal").

## Tallystack (down-market)

- Closer to Ledgerline's segment, $39/seat, but Shopify-only; multi-channel
  support is Ledgerline's wedge against them (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Competitive landscape").

## The real competitor: doing nothing

- Most lost demos buy nothing — the status quo is a spreadsheet plus a
  part-time bookkeeper. The pitch must beat "we manage fine", not just other
  tools (source: source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md,
  "Competitive landscape").
