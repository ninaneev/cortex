# Pricing

## Current position

- Pro is **$79/seat/month for new customers effective 2026-04-01**, raised from
  $59; decided unanimously on 2026-03-04 (source:
  source-docs/2026-03-04-pricing-meeting.md, "Decision").
- Free trial is **14 days** effective 2026-04-01, shortened from 30; 92% of
  converting trials activate within the first 10 days (source:
  source-docs/2026-03-04-pricing-meeting.md, "Decision").

## Grandfathering

- Existing customers (41 accounts) keep $59/seat automatically for 12 months,
  through 2027-04-01, then move to current price with 60 days' notice (source:
  source-docs/2026-03-04-pricing-meeting.md, "Grandfathering").
- Mara emails all existing customers the week of 2026-03-09, before the website
  changes (source: source-docs/2026-03-04-pricing-meeting.md, "Grandfathering").

## Why the raise

- Brightcart deal lost in December 2025; their VP Finance said $59 looked
  "too cheap to be serious" (source: source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md,
  "Pricing signal").
- A ~$30M GMV prospect chose Reconlily in January 2026 on credibility despite
  Ledgerline scoring better in their own evaluation (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Pricing signal").
- Sub-$1M accounts concentrate support cost (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Pricing signal").

## Parked — explicitly not decided

- Annual-only tier: parked until the Q3 2026 pricing review (source:
  source-docs/2026-03-04-pricing-meeting.md, "Parked").
- Usage-based / per-transaction pricing: rejected for 2026 due to metering
  work (source: source-docs/2026-03-04-pricing-meeting.md, "Parked").
