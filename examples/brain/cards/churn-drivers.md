# Churn drivers

## The data

- 7 cancellations in Q4 2025; Mara ran exit calls with all 7 (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Churn — what Q4 taught us").

## Ranked causes

1. **Onboarding failure — 4 of 7.** Customers could not map their chart of
   accounts in the first week and gave up. Called "our biggest fixable
   problem" (source: source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md,
   "Churn — what Q4 taught us").
2. **Missing Amazon settlement report support — 2 of 7.** They reconcile
   Amazon payouts manually and Ledgerline doesn't help (source:
   source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Churn — what Q4 taught us").
3. **Price — 1 of 7.** A sub-$1M GMV account; wrong-fit per the ICP, treated
   as acceptable loss (source: source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md,
   "Churn — what Q4 taught us").

## Fixes in motion

- Guided chart-of-accounts mapping is H1 priority #1, targeting
  time-to-first-reconciliation under 1 day; Sam owns a proposal due
  2026-03-15 (source: source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md,
  "Priorities for H1 2026").
- Amazon settlement report support is H1 priority #2 (source:
  source-docs/2026-02-17-strategy-memo.md, "Priorities for H1 2026").
